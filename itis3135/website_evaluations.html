Website Evaluations

Good Website

  • Contrast: Great contrast. Honestly a white background with black text is objectively good.
  • Repetition: Certain categories repeat such as menswear but not in an obnoxious way.
  • Allignment: The allignment of everything is perfect and pleasing to look at
  • Proximity: The website does a great job addressing proximity. Everything on this website is spaced properly which leads to an easy viewing experience.

Bad Website

 
I assume this website is bad intentionally, however it is still a great example.
  • Contrast: The contrast is not horrible. However, the background art is difficult to look at.
  • Repetition: I've seen Ling's face far too many times within the past 20 seconds of writing this.
  • Allignment: Alignment is not horrible. The main problem is with the vast amount of stuff on my screen
  • Proximity: There is way too much on my screen. I see a million different options all jumbled up together and it makes me want to leave the website.
